# Product Overview

"Where in the World is Nadine Vuan?" is an interactive detective adventure game inspired by Carmen Sandiego. Players take the role of Steve, a job candidate at Caylent, who must track down Nadine Vuan (Talent Recruiter) across 11 global cities by following clues from local informants.

## Core Concept
- Educational geography game with retro 1980s aesthetic
- Detective mystery gameplay with cultural learning elements
- Corporate recruitment theme (Caylent company integration)
- Random starting cities and clue selection for replayability

## Target Audience
- Travel enthusiasts and problem solvers
- Educational game players interested in geography and culture
- Job candidates engaging with Caylent's recruitment process

## Key Features
- 11 cities across 6 continents with unique cultural clues
- Three difficulty levels per city (easy, medium, hard)
- Final destination always Buenos Aires
- Retro educational game art style
- Multilingual elements and cultural authenticity
